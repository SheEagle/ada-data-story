A Two-Stage Classification of Social Distance

How can we decribe social distance?

As we aim to characterize how tone and emotions change with social distance, we subdivide the detected clusters in two stages. First, we define social distance within clusters, and then across clusters.

  • Stage 1: Intra-cluster role differentiation
    Within each cluster, nodes are ranked by their within-cluster weighted degree (strength). Nodes in the top 30% are labeled as Core, while the remaining 70% are labeled as Periphery. This distinction captures whether a mention/edge originates from a structurally central, highly connected position within the cluster or from a more weakly connected, peripheral one.
  • Stage 2: Cross-cluster link strength
    For interactions across clusters, cross-cluster interaction intensity is quantified directly by edge weights. We compute the median weight of all cross-cluster links and apply an 50/50 split. Links above the median are classified as Strong, and those below as Weak, yielding a relative separation between higher and lower intensity cross-cluster connections.

Research Question 02

How do the social attributes of communication links determine baseline linguistic patterns in a peaceful context?

In the previous stage, we defined four types of links: Intra-Core, Intra-Periphery, Cross-Strong, and Cross-Weak. But before we move further with our analysis, we need to take in to account sentiment type and cross-cluster diferences.

Sentiment stratification

Negative references are not evenly distributed across subreddits. Cross-strong links contain a disproportionately high share of negative references, while intra-periphery links contain the fewest; cross-weak and intra-core links show similar levels. Pooling all references would therefore let negative links disproportionately drive the aggregate results. To control for this bias, we analyze negative and positive or neutral references separately.

FIG 2.1

Controling for cluster specific diferences

Each cluster in the Reddit galaxy consists of communities with similar topics, norms, and stylistic habits. These inherent preferences influence language. Some clusters favor long sentences, abstract concepts, and extended reasoning; others prefer short, direct, and pragmatic communication. If a specific interaction type is dominated by certain clusters, cluster-specific style could be misattributed to social distance. To limit this bias, we use a Linear Mixed-Effects Model (LMM). Interaction type is modeled as a fixed effect, using cross-strong links as the reference category. This reduces the influence of cluster-specific language patterns and allows the fixed effects to capture relative differences associated with interaction type.

Research Question 04

Macro Perspective: Steady-State Structures and Conflict-Driven Reorganization in the Reddit Galaxy

So far, we have analyzed communication links at the micro level, examining signal complexity, logic, identity markers, and emotional tone. However, a galaxy is not defined by individual signals alone‚Äîit is shaped by the interplay of stars and clusters. At the macro level, we face a structural question: What fundamentally defines the social architecture of the Reddit galaxy? When conflicts emerge, does this architecture remain stable, or does it reorganize?

Previous analyses were conducted on the global network, considering both positive and negative interactions together. To test the robustness of the observed structures, we reconstructed the galaxy under two extreme observational lenses: one network containing only positive links (peace network) and another containing only negative links (conflict network).

First, we conducted a short network analysis of these 3 networks.

The network audit reveals that the **Peace** sub-network serves as the galaxy‚Äôs structural anchor, mirroring the **Global** skeleton with near-total connectivity (**99.92% LWCC**). In contrast, the **Conflict** network behaves like a dense nebula; despite its smaller footprint, its density is nearly four times higher, yet it suffers from a sharp drop in reciprocity (**0.0675**) and deeper negative assortativity (**-0.1604**). This confirms that while peace facilitates a stable, mutual dialogue that defines the social order, conflict manifests as concentrated, one-sided "firepower coverage" that triggers structural drift across dissimilar clusters.

Figure 4.1 - Structural Fingerprints: While Peace mirrors the Global skeleton, Conflict exhibits a 4x increase in Density and a crash in Reciprocity (0.0675), indicating targeted, non-mutual aggression.

Our methodology involves the following steps:

  1. Extract text and sentiment labels for each interaction from the raw Reddit data.
  2. Construct two separate networks: a peace network (only positive interactions) and a conflict network (only negative interactions).
  3. Compute 27 language features for each link, including readability, sentence length, pronoun usage, emotional tone, and LIWC categories.
  4. Fit a linear mixed-effects model (LMM) to extract fixed-effect coefficients, producing high-dimensional behavior vectors for each link.
  5. Compare peace and conflict contexts using Pearson correlations, Jaccard similarity, and feature drift analysis to track structural stability and role shifts.

1. Initial Hypothesis: ‚ÄúMirror-Constant‚Äù Structures

In the exploratory Phase 0, we observed that positive interactions dominate: roughly 8.35 positive links per negative one. This led to the following hypothesis:

We assume that positive interactions define the Reddit galaxy‚Äôs ‚Äúgravitational skeleton,‚Äù determining which communities occupy the core, which serve as cross-cluster bridges, and which orbit at the periphery. Conflicts may induce local turbulence, but the overall skeletal structure should remain stable.

2. Technical Verification: Peace as the Galaxy‚Äôs Anchor

To test this, we compared the global network with the peace-only network:

  • Computed LMM fixed-effect coefficients for each link, resulting in 27-dimensional high-dimensional behavior vectors.
  • Calculated Pearson correlations between corresponding links in the peace and global networks.
  • Observed near-perfect alignment: peripheral links (Intra-Periphery) reached correlations up to 0.99, with core links similarly aligned.

This confirms that positive interactions do not merely dominate in volume‚Äîthey define the steady-state social architecture. Peace acts as the galaxy‚Äôs gravitational anchor, holding core and peripheral clusters in predictable orbits.

3. Structural Drift: Conflict-Induced Logic Shifts

Examining the conflict-only network reveals that the mirror-constant assumption breaks down. Conflict does not just amplify existing patterns‚Äîit generates a new social gravity, reshaping the galaxy‚Äôs landscape.

Figure 4.5

Correlations shrink significantly under conflict, with peripheral regions showing the largest divergence. This indicates that edge clusters are the first to experience destabilization. Conflict warps the underlying social skeleton, producing behaviors unseen under steady conditions.

4. Role Drift & Structural Reorganization

To analyze structural reorganization under conflict, we employed a combination of set theory and statistical comparison:

  • Jaccard Similarity: Measures the overlap of core members between peace and conflict networks, quantifying role retention or migration.
  • Feature Drift Analysis: For nodes ‚Äúrelegated‚Äù from core to periphery, we track shifts in language features (logic, pronouns, emotional tone, readability) before and after migration, testing the hypothesis that high traffic does not guarantee structural influence.

5. Conflict vs. Peace: Macro Patterns

Peace Defines the Rules

Positive interactions form the framework for clusters, cores, and bridges, which remains robust under normal conditions.

Conflict Drives Drift

Negative interactions trigger structural drift; peripheral clusters destabilize first, and cross-cluster links may lose authority.

Dynamic Role Shifts

High-traffic core links are strong under peace but vulnerable in conflict due to their reliance on positive interactions.

Reshuffling

Conflict elevates peripheral and opportunistic clusters, challenging the established hierarchy.

Conclusion: The Reddit galaxy is a dynamic equilibrium system. Peace defines the steady-state social order, while conflict drives localized reorganization. Influence under calm conditions does not guarantee influence under pure antagonism‚Äîsocial gravity is context-dependent, and roles can invert quickly.
