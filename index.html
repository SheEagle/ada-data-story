Take a Look at Reddit‚Äôs Galaxy

Welcome to the Reddit Galaxy. In this vast digital expanse, every Subreddit acts as a unique planet. Our initial scan revealed two distinct frequencies of communication: Title and Body mentions. As we tracked this galaxy over a 2.5-year period (2014‚Äì2017), we observed a steady, linear expansion ($R^2 = 0.87$). This is not a chaotic growth, but a rhythmic pulse. Perhaps most encouragingly, the "spectral analysis" of these links shows a galaxy dominated by light: Positive sentiment links outnumber negative ones by a staggering 8.35 to 1. Furthermore, positive connections are expanding at a much faster rate (slope 2.39) than negative ones (slope 0.24), indicating that as the Reddit Galaxy grows, it is becoming increasingly defined by constructive alignment rather than friction.

Mapping the Galaxy: From Content Islands to Gravitational Clusters

Based on community social attributes, what distinct communication patterns can we define?

At the early stage of exploring Reddit as a large-scale data galaxy, we initially wanted to perform a clustering on subreddits based on embeddings on user overlap, assuming that if two ‚Äúplanets‚Äù (subreddits) shared a substantial number of active users, they should naturally appear close to each other on the social map. This assumption, however, quickly revealed two critical technical limitations.

  • Structural Isolation: User overlap reflects shared interests rather than actual interaction. Many communities share a large user base yet rarely engage with one another structurally, which artificially pulls together what are effectively parallel worlds.
  • Sparsity & Incompleteness: This embedding approach covers only a subset of communities, leaving large portions of the ecosystem unmapped.

This realization led us to shift focus from surface-level content similarity to a deeper structural layer: the hyperlink-based interaction network. Each mention in a link represents a concrete point of contact between communities. By modeling these links, we wish to construct an inter-community network and identified clusters within it. Each cluster is treated as a ‚Äústar cluster‚Äù: a group of communities with closely aligned norms, topics, and interaction patterns.

Algorithmic Competition: Identifying the Optimal Gravitational Field

To partition tens of thousands of communities into coherent and analytically meaningful structures, we subjected three widely used community detection algorithms to a systematic stress test. Rather than relying on a single metric, we evaluated their performance through three complementary, physically inspired indicators that capture different aspects of structural quality within the Reddit galaxy.

  • üî∏ Conductance: Captures how much interaction ‚Äúleaks‚Äù across cluster boundaries; lower values indicate sharper, more stable borders between star systems.
  • üî∏ Coverage: Indicates the proportion of total interactions in the galaxy that are captured by the detected clusters.

Accordingly, we adopt Leiden as the baseline algorithm for defining cluster-level dynamics in all subsequent analyses.

A Two-Stage Classification of Social Distance

Once this structural map was established, a new challenge emerged: how can we precisely describe ‚Äúsocial distance‚Äù between communities? A simple distinction between ‚Äúwithin-cluster‚Äù and ‚Äúcross-cluster‚Äù interactions is too coarse. It cannot explain why some cross-cluster exchanges remain measured and cooperative, while others escalate rapidly.

To address this, we designed a two-stage classification framework that separates interactions along two independent dimensions: the status of the source community and the intensity of the connection.

  • Stage 1: Intra-cluster role differentiation
    Based on centrality within each star cluster, communities are classified as Core or Periphery. This distinction captures whether a signal originates from a dominant, well-established position or from a more marginal one.
  • Stage 2: Cross-cluster link strength
    For cross-cluster interactions, we compute the median interaction strength and apply a balanced 50/50 split. Links above the median are classified as Strong, representing stable and recurring cross-cluster ties; those below the median are labeled Weak, reflecting infrequent or incidental contact.

Research Question 02

How do the social attributes of communication links determine baseline linguistic patterns in a peaceful context?

In the previous stage, we conceptualized Reddit as a galaxy of tens of thousands of communities and identified four interaction types: Intra-Core, Intra-Periphery, Cross-Strong, and Cross-Weak.

In this section, we move beyond describing the structure itself and ask a more focused question: Once major confounding factors are removed, how does social distance shape language use?

This question seems straightforward but is methodologically delicate. Raw data cannot directly answer it, as they are heavily influenced by covariates. Without proper control, any patterns observed might reflect noise rather than genuine effects. The goal here is not simply to ‚Äúdiscover patterns‚Äù but to systematically calibrate the data and determine which linguistic differences are truly driven by social distance.

üìã Core Terminology

To ensure analytical clarity, we divide interaction contexts into two categories based on the sentiment of links:

  • Peace: Interactions classified as positive or neutral, representing the galaxy‚Äôs steady state.
  • Conflict: Interactions classified as negative, representing periods of instability or disturbance.

First Calibration: Controlling Conflict Rate

The first confounding variable is the uneven distribution of conflict across interaction types.

Conflict is not evenly spread in the Reddit galaxy. Cross-Strong interactions show a much higher frequency of antagonistic language than internal community interactions. By contrast, Intra-Core and Intra-Periphery interactions are generally more restrained. Analyzing all messages together would allow high-conflict language to mask the structural properties of everyday communication.

To address this, we divide the corpus into peaceful and conflictual contexts. In this analysis, we focus first on the peaceful context, where language choices reliably reflect communicative expectations and audience perception.

Second Calibration: Controlling Source Clusters

Even within peaceful interactions, a second confound exists: the Source Cluster.

Each cluster in the Reddit galaxy consists of communities with similar topics, norms, and stylistic habits. These inherent preferences influence language. Some clusters favor long sentences, abstract concepts, and extended reasoning; others prefer short, direct, and pragmatic communication. If a specific interaction type is dominated by certain clusters, cluster-specific style could be misattributed to social distance.

To remove this bias, we apply a Linear Mixed-Effects Model (LMM). Each message‚Äôs source cluster is treated as a random effect, absorbing cluster-level stylistic differences. The remaining fixed effects therefore more reliably reflect the influence of interaction type itself. Effectively, the LMM allows us to observe language in the Reddit galaxy after subtracting local cultural coloration.

Research Question 03

If the Reddit galaxy under peaceful conditions revealed an elegant equilibrium shaped by stable orbits, conflict acts as a sudden gravitational disturbance. Using the LMM model on conflict-context data, we observed that antagonism does not merely amplify emotions‚Äîit fundamentally reshapes the structure of signals across the galaxy.

We focus on Cross-Strong links‚Äîthe connections that, even during disputes, attempt to maintain a form of ‚Äúdiplomatic stability‚Äù‚Äîto track how social distance distorts communication under conflict.

Research Question 04

Macro Perspective: Steady-State Structures and Conflict-Driven Reorganization in the Reddit Galaxy

Up until now, we‚Äôve explored how different communication links behave at the micro-level, analyzing signal complexity, logic, identity markers, and emotional tone. But a galaxy isn‚Äôt defined by individual signals alone‚Äîit is the interplay of all its stars and clusters. At a macro scale, we face a structural question: What fundamentally defines the social architecture of the Reddit galaxy? When conflicts erupt, does this architecture remain resilient, or does it collapse and reorganize?

To address this, we adopt a multi-dimensional LMM coefficient vector framework, which allows us to track the stability and divergence of interaction patterns across different social contexts. This framework provides a way to map the galaxy‚Äôs structural ‚Äúgravity wells‚Äù and observe how they shift under conflict.

1. Initial Hypothesis: ‚ÄúMirror-Constant‚Äù Structures

In the exploratory Phase 0, we observed that the Reddit galaxy is dominated by positive interactions: roughly 8.35 positive links for every negative one. This led to an intuitive hypothesis:

Positive interactions define the social skeleton of the galaxy. They determine who occupies the core, who bridges clusters, and who orbits the periphery. Conflicts may add turbulence, but they should not fundamentally reshape this skeleton‚Äîat most, they change the ‚Äúcolor‚Äù of the network without altering its structure.

In other words, the galaxy‚Äôs structural backbone, formed by the majority of positive links, was expected to remain mirror-constant, stable across both friendly and adversarial interactions.

2. Technical Verification: Peace as the Galaxy‚Äôs ‚ÄúAnchor‚Äù

To test this, we compared two perspectives: Global Perspective (including all interactions) and Purely Positive Perspective (filtering out conflicts).

Methodology & Observations

We extracted the fixed-effect coefficients for 27 language features, producing high-dimensional behavior vectors for each link. Computing Pearson correlation coefficients revealed near-perfect alignment: peripheral links (Intra-Periphery) demonstrate correlations as high as 0.99.

This implies that positive interactions are not just dominant in volume‚Äîthey define the steady-state social architecture of the Reddit galaxy. Peace acts as the galaxy‚Äôs gravitational anchor, holding core and peripheral clusters in predictable orbits.

3. Structural Divergence: Conflict-Induced Logic Shifts

When we shift to a purely negative perspective, the mirror-constant assumption breaks. Conflict does not simply amplify existing patterns‚Äîit creates a new social gravity, one that reshapes the galaxy‚Äôs landscape.

Structural Drift: High-dimensional behavior vectors losing alignment under pure conflict contexts.

Observations show that correlations shrink significantly under conflict. Peripheral regions (Intra-Periphery) show the largest divergence, indicating that clusters at the edge of the galaxy are the first to experience structural destabilization. Conflict warps the underlying social skeleton, producing behaviors unseen under steady conditions.

4. Role Reorganization: Deceptiveness of High-Traffic Links

Why do seemingly dominant core links lose influence in conflict? LMM intercept and coefficient shifts reveal that high traffic does not guarantee structural power.

Relegation

Core Cross-Strong links appear central in global models due to sheer interaction volume, but lose influence in specific contexts.

Bubble Effect

Their influence depends on a majority of non-negative interactions‚Äîessentially a traffic bubble that bursts under conflict.

Reshuffling

In a pure conflict context, previously peripheral connections rise, demonstrating structural role reshuffling.

5. Conflict vs. Peace: Macro Patterns

Peace Defines the Rules

Positive interactions established the structural framework of clusters, cores, and bridges. This framework is remarkably resilient under normal conditions.

Conflict Drives Divergence

Negative interactions trigger structural drift. Peripheral clusters destabilize first, and cross-cluster links may lose authority.

Dynamic Role Shifts

High-traffic core links are powerful under peace but are vulnerable to conflict due to their reliance on positive interactions.

Reshuffling

The conflict environment elevates peripheral and opportunistic clusters, challenging the established hierarchy.

Conclusion: The Reddit galaxy is a dynamic equilibrium system, where peace defines the steady-state social order, and conflict drives localized reorganization. Influence in calm conditions does not guarantee influence under pure antagonism‚Äîsocial gravity is context-sensitive, and roles can invert quickly.
